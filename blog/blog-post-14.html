Is Cybersecurity Important?

Threats through Phishing, Ransomware, Decoy, Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) cyber attacks are increasing and more sophisticated.

As these threats become increasingly sophisticated, and may even affect an organization's own reputation, it is progressively important to look at the cybersecurity professional as an indispensable ally in the protection of data hosted on the network.

It is difficult to talk about this topic without bringing to the fore the impact that the pandemic has had on our lives. The need for almost instantaneous adaptation to successive confinements was transversal to all sectors and, at a time when, overnight, many had to access VPN networks through home, exposure was boosted, facilitating hacker attacks.

Cybersecurity, an area that has actually existed for some time, has come to be seen as the solution to protect companies, and the search for professionals in this area has begun to grow. In fact, the most recent publications published regarding the professions with higher demand and more competitive salaries, place this activity in the top 10. So I have no doubt that this is a trend that will move to the foreground for many companies in Portugal.

Portugal should define a strategy to raise awareness of this theme, with a view to combating the lack of information from society, and the companies themselves. And I believe the road really goes through here. Training, training and training, throughout the country.

Given that we must continue to take remote work as a reality, there is a growing challenge in finding and hiring professionals fit for the protection of companies. The fact that there is a shortage of talent in Portugal also does not help in this demand. However, society is beginning to realize the importance of this theme and the competitive advantage lies precisely there.

This is the time, therefore, to look to the future. The possible functions in the area of cybersecurity are almost endless – and the need to occupy them as well. We have as examples Data Analyst, Forensic Analyst, Incident Response Technician, Database Administrator. But the list continues and reveals an area of constant growth in a sector with an atypical demand (positive mind) and with a level of wages above normal.


The Wrap Up

As such, it is essential that schools, regardless of the teaching method they adopt, promote cyber-education in order to ensure a smiling future for businesses and society itself.
