What are the most sought Technology careers and how to start?

Take a look around you. In fact, look at whatever you’re using to read this article. Whether it is a tablet, smartphone, or computer, there’s no doubt that technology is all around us these days.


Best Technology Careers


Full-Stack Developer
A full-stack developer is a developer who can manage both frontend and backend development. They create server-side and client-side functions, such as websites and databases. Full-stack developers have knowledge of multiple programming languages, database technologies, and API development, to name a few.

DevOps Engineer
DevOps engineers test and build software with automation capabilities. They take care of managing resources, monitoring the performance of software updates, and overseeing network operations.

Data Scientist
With the immense volume of data traveling to, from, and within organizations, more and more businesses are relying on data scientists to help gain insights from raw data. Data scientists analyze and present information such that business leaders and people can make better decisions.

IoT Solutions Architect
An Internet of Things (IoT) solutions architect is responsible for working with sales teams and engineers to establish efficient processes using the aid of technology. The solutions architect may be tasked with getting the buy-in from executives to deploy a new technology within an organization, for example. This is a great role for someone who enjoys the blend of business, technology, and strategy.

Cybersecurity Analyst
With the rise of technological systems and applications, the threat of security breaches is only growing. That’s why there’s a huge demand for cybersecurity analysts and specialists. Cybersecurity analysts protect a company’s software, hardware, and networks from threats and breaches. They need to understand how the infrastructure is set up and evaluate risks to plan for protection and prevention.



How to Start a Career in Technology?


If you’re interested in a career in technology, then you can follow these steps to get going:


Earn a degree or certificate
Most positions will require at least a bachelor’s degree. You can obtain a degree in subjects like computer science, information technology, or a related field to work in positions in tech. Or, if you already have a job in the field and are looking to expand your skills and knowledge, you can enroll in a certificate program.

Develop your skills
Whether you have earned your degree already or are in the process, you should always work on developing and strengthening your skills. As mentioned, this could be done by taking a certificate program. Or, it could be by enrolling in a boot camp dedicated to your field of choice, or by practicing on your own time.

Expand experience
Gain experience through freelance gigs, internships, and even paid positions. For roles like web developers, it’s great to build a portfolio to showcase your work. For other positions, you can expand your network through your experiences so you have references when it comes time to apply for your dream job.

Apply to jobs
With education and experience, it’s time to apply for jobs! You’ll need to prepare a resume or CV and get ready to interview.
